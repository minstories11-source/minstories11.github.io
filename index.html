Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the free version?
The free version includes ALL core features: unlimited bookmarks, pins, tags, collections, analytics, trash bin, dark mode, bulk operations, and import/export. The only difference is ads are shown on this website to support development.
Are there ads in the browser extension?
No! The extension itself is completely ad-free for all users. Ads only appear on this promotional website. Premium removes ads from this website and provides additional benefits.
Can I cancel my Premium subscription?
Yes, you can cancel anytime with no penalties. Your Premium access remains active until the end of your current billing period. We also offer a 7-day money-back guarantee if you're not satisfied.
Is my data private and secure?
Absolutely! All bookmark data is stored locally on your device. We don't collect, track, or sell your data. Premium subscriptions are processed securely through Stripe, and we never see your payment information.
How do I get support?
